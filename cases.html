Cloud-based
music player

Zonga is a Romanian music streaming service developed by Trilulilu. Launched in 2012, the service quickly became the most important local music service, with over 22 million songs available for listening. In 2011 they came to us looking for a mobile development team that would be able to develop their complex service into two native apps – one for Android and one for iOS. They started working with us right away trusting us with all the stages of the project, from product concept and wireframing to design and development.
  • Product concept
  • UI & UX guidance
  • Graphic design
  • Front-end development

The app we developed by tapptitude, a mobile application development agency based in Cluj-Napoca, Romania and includes all the features found in a typical music streaming application and some more! For instance, the app allows its users to download their favourite playlists so they can listen to their music offline. However, the music files had to be encrypted, and as a result, we ended up developing a custom media player. Another neat feature is the automatic synchronisation that allows one’s music to be synced over the cloud between all devices.

«The quality of our apps always needs to be state of the art. Our customers pay for the service because they love music and thus expect a great listening experience. The Factorly team was the only one that was able to deliver on our high expectations in terms of development quality, deadline meeting and reliability. Working with them has been a breeze and I would recommend them to anyone looking for a serious mobile development company.»

Sergiu Biris

CEO of Zonga
